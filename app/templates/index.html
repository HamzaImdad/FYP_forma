How It Works

Smart Analysis for
Every Rep

01

Pose Detection

MediaPipe BlazePose tracks 33 body landmarks in real time from your webcam feed.

02

Form Analysis

Joint angles, body ratios and symmetry metrics are computed every frame for precise evaluation.

03

AI Classification

Dedicated exercise detectors with state machines, plus rule-based, Random Forest and BiLSTM classifiers evaluate your form in real time.

04

Live Feedback

Colour-coded skeleton overlay and real-time text cues tell you exactly what to fix, rep by rep.

Why ExerVision

Your AI Personal Trainer

Real-Time Processing

Processes frames at high FPS with GPU acceleration. No perceptible lag between your movement and the on-screen feedback — evaluation happens within milliseconds of each frame capture.

Joint-Level Accuracy

Evaluates individual joint positions with colour-coded feedback — green for correct alignment, orange for minor issues, and red for positions that need immediate correction.

Session Reports

Detailed post-workout breakdown with per-rep form scores, bar charts of your performance over time, and a ranked list of your most common form mistakes to target in your next session.

No Equipment Needed

Works with just your webcam — no wearables, no sensors, no expensive hardware. Any laptop or desktop with a camera is all you need to get professional-grade form analysis.

Guided Instructions

Each exercise includes step-by-step form guidance, targeted muscle groups, and a curated list of common mistakes to watch out for — so you know what good form looks like before you start.

Rep Counting

Automatic repetition detection using peak-based motion analysis, with per-rep form scoring and a colour-coded history so you can track consistency across your entire set.

        About the Project

        Smarter Training,
        Better Results

        A real-time computer vision system for exercise form evaluation. Built as a BSc Computer Science final year project at the University of Greenwich.

        The Problem

        Poor Form Leads to Injuries

        Incorrect exercise form is the leading cause of gym injuries. Personal trainers are expensive and not always available. Most people train alone without feedback on their technique, risking long-term damage to joints and muscles that could easily be prevented with real-time guidance.

        The Solution

        AI That Watches Your Form

        ExerVision uses computer vision and machine learning to analyse your body position in real time, providing the same feedback a personal trainer would — instantly and for free. Just open your webcam, select an exercise, and start training with confidence.
